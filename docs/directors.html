About the Executive Committee

The Homecoming Executive Committee (HEC) is composed of an Executive Officer, two Officers, and eleven Directors. Each Officer oversees five to six Directors, and each Director leads a committee of Captains and Committee Members.

Throughout the year, the members of the Homecoming Executive Committee plan and organize all Homecoming events, playing a vital role in the success of Penn State’s Homecoming celebration. Every member of the Executive Committee is a current University Park student.

Meet the Executive Committee

Executive Director

Homecoming 2026

Zach McGuire is a senior from Williamsport, PA, studying Architectural Engineering and will be serving as the Executive Director for Homecoming 2026. McGuire has been a part of Homecoming since his freshman year, starting in the First Year Involvement Committee. He served as an Operations Captain for two years, as well as the Operations Director for Homecoming 2025. This year, he is excited to bring the community together for Homecoming 2026.
