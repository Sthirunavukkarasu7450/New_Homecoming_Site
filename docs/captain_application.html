Captain Application

Captain Responsibilities Include:
  1. Must be available during the fall semester (2026)
  2. Weekly committee meetings and Director one-on-one meetings
  3. Participate in multiple Homecoming fundraisers (i.e. Tailgate Ambassadors, BJC concession stands, Stadium Cleanup, etc.)
  4. Maintain an active presence at ALL Homecoming Week events
  5. Write weekly, mid-year, and final reports and participate in year-end wrap up meetings
  6. Participate in collaborative groups and work closely with other Directors and Captains
  7. Attend all Homecoming events (internal workshops, committee events, and Homecoming events)
  8. Actively engage in recruitment of committee members
  9. Assist other committees during Homecoming week (assist with security on parade day if you do not have any other assigned responsibilities)
